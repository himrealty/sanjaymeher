From Passion to Profession

From a young age, I was fascinated with technology and creativity. This passion led me to learn video editing, which soon turned into a profession. I started freelancing, earning extra money while pursuing my studies and full-time job.

This encouraged me to learn other related skills like Graphic Design, WordPress Website Design, Digital Marketing, and AI.

With an experience of over 7 years in Video Editing & Graphic Design and 2 years in Digital Marketing, I've worked for multiple clients across diverse niches — Gaming, Tech, Food, Real Estate, Fashion, Education.

I started my agency Graphicy in 2023 to help people achieve the same.
